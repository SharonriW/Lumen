About Lumen · 关于我们

Built on the belief
that clarity is kindness. 我们相信：清晰，即是善意

Surrogacy is one of the most significant decisions a family will ever make. Lumen exists to make that process honest, supported, and legally sound — from the first question to the last form.

代孕是家庭最重要的决定之一。Lumen 的使命，是让这一过程诚实、有支撑、且在法律上无懈可击——从第一个问题到最后一份文件。

Our values · 我们的价值观
Three principles that guide every decision. · 三项原则，指引每一个决定。
01 — Transparency · 透明

No surprises.
Ever.永远不留惊喜

Every cost, every timeline, every possible complication — we tell you upfront. Clarity is not a feature, it is the foundation of trust.

每项费用、每个时间节点、每种可能的并发症——我们提前告知。清晰不是附加功能，而是信任的基础。

02 — Respect · 尊重

Surrogates are
partners, not vessels.代孕妈妈是伙伴，不是工具

We hold surrogates to high standards because we hold them in high regard. Their health, autonomy, and wellbeing are never subordinated to the intended parents' timeline.

我们对代孕妈妈要求严格，正因为我们极度尊重她们。她们的健康与自主权永远不会屈从于委托方的时间表。

03 — Rigor · 严谨

Thorough process.
Protected outcomes.严谨流程，保障结果

We work with experienced reproductive attorneys, licensed clinical psychologists, and established IVF centers. Shortcuts don't exist here.

我们与经验丰富的生殖律师、持牌心理医生及知名IVF诊所合作。我们不走捷径——严谨的流程是可靠结果的保证。

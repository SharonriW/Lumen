About Lumen

Built on the belief
that clarity is kindness.

Surrogacy is one of the most significant decisions a family will ever make. Lumen exists to make that process honest, supported, and legally sound — from the first question to the last form.

Our values
Three principles that guide every decision.
01 — Transparency

No surprises.
Ever.

Every cost, every timeline, every possible complication — we tell you upfront. Clarity is not a feature, it is the foundation of trust. We would rather give you an uncomfortable truth early than a comfortable uncertainty later.

02 — Respect

Surrogates are
partners, not vessels.

We hold surrogates to high standards because we hold them in high regard. Their health, autonomy, and wellbeing are never subordinated to the intended parents' timeline. A good match means both sides feel genuinely seen.

03 — Rigor

Thorough process.
Protected outcomes.

We work with experienced reproductive attorneys, licensed clinical psychologists, and established IVF centers. Shortcuts don't exist here. The rigor of our process is what makes the outcome reliable.

Compliance & credentials
Legal & professional standards

Every safeguard,
in place.

Lumen operates within California's established legal and ethical framework for third-party reproduction. We hold ourselves to standards that go beyond what is required.

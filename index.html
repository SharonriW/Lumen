Why California · 为什么选择加州

The gold standard
for surrogacy law. 代孕法律的黄金标准

California is the only U.S. state that reliably issues pre-birth orders for all family configurations — married, single, same-sex, or international. Your name is on the birth certificate from day one.

加州是全美唯一对所有家庭结构均可发出出生前法令的州——已婚、单身、同性或国际家庭均受保护。

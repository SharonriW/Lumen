Apply to become a surrogate · 申请成为代孕妈妈
  • Between 21 and 40 years of age
  • Have previously given birth to at least one child you are parenting
  • Healthy BMI (typically 18–32, varies by clinic)
  • Non-smoker; no illegal drug use in the past 12 months
  • No major uncontrolled health conditions
  • U.S. resident with stable living situation
  • No history of significant mental health conditions (cleared through psychological evaluation)
  • Willing to carry for intended parents regardless of family type
  • Not currently receiving government financial assistance
  • Have a reliable support system (partner, family, or friends)

From application to match
The process

What happens after you apply.

We move at a pace that feels right to you. Our screening process is thorough because it protects everyone — including you.

01

Initial application

Complete a brief online form with your health history, family situation, and motivations. Our team reviews every application personally.

02

Screening call

A 30–45 minute call with our team to ask questions, understand the process, and ensure it's the right fit for your life right now.

03

Medical & psychological evaluation

A full medical screening at your IVF clinic, plus a psychological evaluation with a licensed counselor experienced in third-party reproduction. All costs covered.

04

Profile creation & matching

We create your profile and begin presenting it to compatible intended parent families. You review profiles and decide who feels right — no pressure.

05

Legal contract

An independent attorney represents you (at no cost to you) throughout the contract process. All terms are fully explained before you sign anything.

06

Transfer & pregnancy support

Your dedicated case manager is available throughout the entire pregnancy. We're here for the clinical coordination, the emotional moments, and everything in between.
